The Gifts

This project is a 6 minute horror film which I made when I was in second year in university, it’s name is The Gifts. The film is about a mean girl who stole her best friend’s boyfriend and then meets a weird clown on the street. Then some weird things happened after. My part: I participated in the whole process of making this film. This was the first time I could make a whole film, so I was very passionate. The main part of mine was the post-production part, I basically did the whole post-production. I am really proud of the effects I did for this film.

Process

At the beginning, we all agreed to make a horror film. And then we started to brainstorm and do research. It was very hard to decide an actual theme for the film, because we needed to think every detail in order to make sure we wouldn’t meet other difficulties after. After lots of discussions, we decided to make something about gifts and transmit some information to audiences from the film.
The shooting process was not that well-off, looking for the house was the biggest problem. Fortunately we solved it. Because we did not do a very good job to prepare, the shooting process was not that easy. When we did the actual shooting, we found some of the parts were impossible to shoot. So we needed to change the script.

Reflection

This was my first time participating in making a whole film, I think the whole process could be better, but we tried the best of ourselves. From this experience, I learned that we have to do the preparations as thoroughly as possible, otherwise, we will meet many problems when we do the actual shooting. Also, we need to pay more attention to the audio part, especially when recording, Because it is very hard to modify after.
