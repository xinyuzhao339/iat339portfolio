Our Endangered Animals

This project is a proposal of a children’s book. The book is called Our Endangered Animals. This is a publishing course in which we need to know the whole process of publishing a book and make a whole proposal is the most effective way to achieve this.
I participated in the whole project, from choosing the topic to the final step. However, the part I was really responsible for was graphic design of course. I made the cover page and sample pages of the inside page.

Process

We spend lots of time on deciding the theme. After lots of research, we found children’s books are very interesting and worth doing, so we decided to make a proposal about a children’s book which introduces endangered animals. Deciding the style of the inside page was very difficult too. Since it is a children’s book, illustrations are a big part. Finding the appropriate illustrations was also not easy.
After deciding what illustrations we would use, I tried lots of design styles. At first, I made sample pages which contained lots of bright colors, but they were too colorful and very distracting. After I tried so many times, I finally found the style we wanted and I think the outcome is not bad.

Reflection

This was a very pleasant experience, because the whole process was very relaxing and no pressure at all. This is not common in SIAT. Also, this was my first time trying to make something for children. I was very lost at the beginning, fortunately I found an appropriate style. I think trying to make different styles of design is very important for designers, otherwise, our mind will be constrained.
